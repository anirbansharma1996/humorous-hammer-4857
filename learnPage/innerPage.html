What are product experience insights?

Product experience insights are data that helps product management teams understand exactly how
customers are experiencing a product. As you focus on PX insights, you can improve user experience

‚ùó User experience (UX) is a subcategory of CX that covers how users interact with a specific entity,

‚ùó Product experience (PX) looks specifically at how users interact with a digital product and is
often‚Äîbut not exclusively‚Äîthe focus of product teams in the SaaS (Software-as-a-Service)
